Consultation FAQ

Common questions about scheduling and conducting consultations with our team.

How long does a consultation take?

Initial consultations typically last 45-60 minutes. We want to ensure there's adequate time to discuss your goals and answer all your questions thoroughly.

Can I bring my spouse or financial advisor?

Absolutely! We encourage you to include anyone who's part of your investment decision-making process. We want everyone to be informed and comfortable.

Are consultations available outside business hours?

Yes, we can arrange consultations on evenings and weekends by special arrangement. Just let us know your preferred times in your request.

Is there any cost for the consultation?

No, initial consultations are completely free. There's no obligation to join, and we view this as a mutual evaluation to determine if we're a good fit.

How quickly can I schedule a consultation?

We typically respond to requests within 1-2 business days and can often schedule consultations within the same week, depending on availability.

What if I'm not ready to invest immediately?

That's perfectly fine! We're happy to discuss our approach and answer questions even if you're in the early stages of exploring investment options.
