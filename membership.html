Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about membership and The Houston 100 investment approach.

What is the time commitment for members?

Members are expected to participate in quarterly Growth & Vision sessions and stay engaged with the community. Beyond that, involvement is flexible based on your interests and availability.

How does voting and governance work?

Members have voting rights on key decisions with voting power based on participation level. Our governance structure ensures transparency and member input on strategic direction.

What makes The Houston 100 different?

Our combination of strategic real estate focus, principled leadership, community impact emphasis, and comprehensive member platform creates a unique investment experience.

What about investment minimums and fees?

Investment details including minimums and fee structures are discussed during the consultation process to ensure we can tailor our approach to your specific situation and goals.

Can I bring my spouse or advisor to the consultation?

Absolutely! We encourage you to include anyone who is part of your investment decision-making process. We want everyone to be comfortable and informed.

Is there any obligation from the consultation?

No obligation whatsoever. The consultation is a mutual evaluation to determine if The Houston 100 is a good fit for your investment goals and if you're a good fit for our community.
