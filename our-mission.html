Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Houston 100 Investment Group LLC is committed to three core principles:

  • Empowering Individuals: Providing accessible investment opportunities and comprehensive financial education to create generational wealth.
  • Building Wealth: Implementing strategic, long-term investment strategies to generate sustainable returns for our members.
  • Community Impact: Leveraging our resources and success to create positive change in our community while maintaining Biblical principles.

The Houston 100 Objective

To build Kingdom-focused wealth through 100 strategic investment doors, governed by Biblical principles, creating sustainable returns for members while empowering individuals through financial education and generating meaningful community impact.

Each investment represents a strategic gateway:

  • A door to community transformation
  • A door to generational wealth
  • A door to Kingdom advancement
  • A door to Biblical stewardship
  • A door to economic empowerment

Our Investment Philosophy

Houston 100 focuses on strategic real estate investments that provide both financial returns and community benefit. Our approach combines traditional investment analysis with Kingdom impact assessment.

We believe in creating value through:

  • Comprehensive due diligence processes
  • Market analysis and trend identification
  • Risk management and mitigation strategies
  • Long-term value creation focus
  • Community benefit evaluation

Investment Focus Areas

Our primary investment focus centers on strategic real estate opportunities with strong fundamentals and community impact potential.

  • Real Estate Development: Multi-family residential properties, single-family developments, and commercial opportunities
  • Market Securities: Dividend-focused stocks, growth stocks with strong fundamentals, and diversified ETFs
  • Community Programs: Our flagship DHAP program helps families avoid foreclosure while creating investment opportunities
  • Future Expansion: Private equity, local business investments, and alternative investment opportunities
