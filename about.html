Who We Are

Houston 100 Investment Group LLC is a Kingdom-focused real estate investment company founded on Biblical principles and committed to creating generational wealth while serving our community.

We believe that financial success and Kingdom impact go hand in hand. Our approach combines professional investment expertise with a heart for service, creating opportunities that benefit both our members and the communities we serve.

What We Do

We specialize in strategic real estate investments with a focus on sustainable returns and community benefit. Our portfolio includes residential development, commercial opportunities, and innovative programs like DHAP.

  • Strategic real estate acquisition and development
  • Community-focused investment programs
  • Member education and financial empowerment
  • Foreclosure prevention through DHAP
